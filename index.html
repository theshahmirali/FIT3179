What is Covid-19?

The Covid-19 is an ongoing pandemic of corona virus disease caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS-CoV-2). It was first identified in Wuhan, China in December, 2019. The outbreak was declared as pandemic in March 2020. As of 1 November 2020 more than 46.1 million cases have been confirmed resulting in 1.19 million deaths.
